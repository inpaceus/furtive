Constitution

While it remains the law, unanimously applicable, any legislative body must function open to interpretation; rules carried out by the staff.

1

Be a good person.

Do not engage in acts of deliberate harm to other members, or the server as an institution itself. You must conduct yourself mindful of the other people, and not be exploitative.

2

Comply with staff.

If a staffmember directs you to do something, you should most definitely listen. Activity which is offensive, against the warnings of our staff, or intended to irritate will result in punishment.

3

Remain secure.

The account you connect with is always your responsibility. In the event that someone causes harm by gaining your credentials, you must be held accountable. We encourage security.

File a report

If you've become victim of one who has broken our policies, or would like to tip us on a fellow member, you may file a report.
